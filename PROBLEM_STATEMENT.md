# Problem Statement: The Healthcare Financial Crisis in India

## 1. The Context: A Nation at Risk
Despite the rapid digitization of India through the "India Stack" (UPI, ONDC, ABDM), a critical gap remains: **Financial Protection in Healthcare.** While access to healthcare is expanding, the ability of the average Indian family to afford it is shrinking.

### The Catastrophic Data (NSSO & World Bank)
- **55 Million Indians** are pushed below the poverty line every year due to medical expenses.
- **Out-of-Pocket Expenditure (OOPE)** in India stands at **39.4%**, significantly higher than the global average.
- **1 in 3 Indian families** (31.6%) faces catastrophic health expenditure annually.

## 2. The Three Structural Failures

### A. The Information Asymmetry (Schemes)
There are **200+ Central and State health schemes** (PM-JAY, RSBY, State Arogyasri, etc.). However, there is no unified, intelligent way for a citizen to know which ones they qualify for. Complexity in documentation and eligibility criteria acts as a barrier to entry for the most vulnerable.

### B. The "Hospital Markup" Crisis (Bill Padding)
Private hospital bills often include markups of **500% to 1500%** on medical consumables like gloves, syringes, and IV sets. Phantom charges and unbundled billing practices are common. Citizens have no benchmark to compare their bills against national standards like the **CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme) rate cards**.

### C. The Branded Drug Monopoly
Branded medicines in India often cost **5x to 10x** more than their generic equivalents. While the government has launched 18,000+ **Jan Aushadhi (PMBJP)** stores offering medicines at 90% discount, the "Missing Middle" (middle class) lacks an easy way to map their branded prescriptions to generic alternatives.

## 3. The AarogyaRaksha Solution
AarogyaRaksha is built as a **DPI Intelligence Layer** to solve these three failures through AI-driven advocacy.

1. **AI Scheme Matcher**: Uses Gemini AI to reason across 200+ policy databases and match citizens instantly based on their ABHA profile.
2. **Vision-Based Bill Auditor**: Uses Multimodal AI to scan hospital bills and flag overcharging items by comparing them to CGHS benchmarks.
3. **Medicine Optimizer**: A direct bridge to the PMBJP Jan Aushadhi network, identifying generic alternatives and calculating lifetime savings.
